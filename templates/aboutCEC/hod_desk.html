The Department of Electrical Engineering at Chaibasa Engineering College offers a vibrant environment for undergraduate education and research in Electrical Engineering since 2013. Chaibasa Engineering College was established in the year 2013 and the Department of Electrical Engineering has been one of the major departments of the Institute. The department began with fifty-six undergraduate students and three faculty members; currently, the department has nine faculty members, and one ninety undergraduate students. In a very short span of time, the department was enhanced excellent learning environment with dedicated young faculty members, technical staff, excellent laboratories and innovative academic processes. In order to perform the complex problem in modern applications of Electrical Engineering, the department has a well combination of hardware and software facilities. Apart from this department of Electrical Engineering had a leading role for the upliftment of rural community and as well as the institution. The department conduct a B.Tech program affiliated with Jharkhand University of Technology. The entire program has been approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). Our department is ISO 9001:2015 certified. Most students get placement in leading industries or organizations and get admission to premium institutions in India & abroad for their higher studies.
