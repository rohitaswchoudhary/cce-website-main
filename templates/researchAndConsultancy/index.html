About

The committee promotes enhancement of research output of the college. It makes the members of the faculty informed of the schemes of UGC and other funding agencies. The committee also makes sure that the proposals are submitted in time and proper follow up actions are made. It also makes invaluable suggestions for the improvement of the research centres of the college. The committee plans to launch a programme to encourage the PG students to engage more in research projects.
